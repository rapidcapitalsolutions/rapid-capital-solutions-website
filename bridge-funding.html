What is bridge funding?

Bridge financing covers a short gap until longer-term funding, a receivable, or seasonal revenue arrives. Terms are typically 3–24 months with flexible early payoff on select programs.

When businesses use bridge capital

  • Contract start-up costs before first payment
  • Emergency equipment or facility repairs
  • Inventory opportunities with tight deadlines
  • Payroll during unexpected slowdowns
  • Gap between closing on a deal and permanent financing

How fast can bridge funding close?

Many bridge and working capital files fund in 24–48 hours after documents are received. Larger amounts may take slightly longer.
