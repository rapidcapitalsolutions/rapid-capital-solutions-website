Programs

Funding built for how you actually run your business

From short-term working capital to long-term growth financing — we match you with the right product, not a one-size-fits-all offer.

◆

Working Capital

Fast access to cash for payroll, inventory, marketing, or any operational need. Revenue-based repayment that flexes with your deposits.

  • Amount: $5K – $500K
  • Term: 3 – 18 months
  • Speed: Same-day to 48 hours
◇

Merchant Cash Advance

Advance against future card and bank deposits. Ideal for businesses with strong daily or weekly revenue flow.

  • Amount: $10K – $1M
  • Term: 4 – 24 months
  • Speed: 24 – 72 hours
○

Business Line of Credit

Draw funds when you need them, pay interest only on what you use. Revolving access for ongoing cash flow management.

  • Amount: $10K – $250K
  • Term: 12 – 24 months
  • Speed: 1 – 3 business days
□

Term Business Loan

Fixed monthly payments for expansion, acquisition, or major capital projects. Longer terms with predictable repayment.

  • Amount: $25K – $5M
  • Term: 12 – 60 months
  • Speed: 3 – 10 business days
△

Equipment Financing

Finance trucks, machinery, restaurant equipment, medical devices, and more. The asset often secures the deal.

  • Amount: $10K – $500K
  • Term: 24 – 72 months
  • Speed: 2 – 5 business days
◎

Invoice / AR Financing

Unlock cash tied up in outstanding invoices. B2B businesses with net-30 or net-60 clients can access funds before customers pay.

  • Amount: $25K – $2M
  • Term: Revolving
  • Speed: 3 – 7 business days
◈

SBA-Style Programs

Lower-rate, longer-term structures for qualified businesses. Strong credit and documentation typically required.

  • Amount: $50K – $5M
  • Term: 60 – 120 months
  • Speed: 2 – 6 weeks
◉

Revenue-Based Financing

Flexible repayment tied to monthly revenue. Great for seasonal businesses or companies with variable cash flow.

  • Amount: $15K – $750K
  • Term: 6 – 36 months
  • Speed: 1 – 3 business days
★

Renewal & Stacking

Already funded elsewhere? We specialize in renewals, add-on capital, and stacking positions when your revenue supports it.

  • Amount: Varies by position
  • Term: Custom
  • Speed: 24 – 48 hours
